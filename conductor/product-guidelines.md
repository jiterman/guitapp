# Product Guidelines: GuitApp

## Communication Style & Prose
- **Tone**: Friendly and supportive. We want users to feel encouraged about managing their finances, not overwhelmed.
- **Language**: Use conversational but clear language. Use "we" to refer to the app and "you" to address the user.
- **Feedback**: Provide positive reinforcement when tasks are completed (e.g., "Great! You've logged your first expense.").

## Visual Branding & UI
- **Aesthetic**: Vibrant and playful. Use a bold color palette to make the experience engaging and less "boring" than typical financial apps.
- **Interface**: Keep layouts structured but use playful elements like icons and subtle animations to enhance the experience.
- **Icons**: Use a consistent, rounded icon set that matches the vibrant theme.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Speed & Efficiency**: Designing for the "on-the-go" user. Logging an expense should be possible in under 5 seconds.
- **Clarity & Readability**: Information should be glanceable. Use clear typography and high-contrast elements for important data.
- **Familiarity & Consistency**: Follow standard mobile UI patterns (e.g., Tab bar navigation, Material Design/Human Interface Guidelines) to ensure users feel at home immediately.

## Accessibility
- **Standards**: Adhere to basic accessibility standards, focusing on high color contrast for text and ensuring touch targets are appropriately sized (min 44x44 dp).
- **Navigation**: Ensure the app is navigable via standard assistive technologies like screen readers.
