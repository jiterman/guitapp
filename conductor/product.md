# Product Definition: GuitApp

## Vision
GuitApp is a mobile expense management application designed for the general public. It aims to simplify personal finance by providing a frictionless way to track daily spending and gain valuable financial insights through intuitive summaries and smart alerts.

## Target Audience
- **General Public**: Individuals seeking a straightforward and efficient tool to manage their personal finances.

## Key Goals
- **Easy Tracking**: Minimize the effort required to record daily expenses.
- **Financial Insights**: Provide clear visual reports to help users understand their spending patterns.
- **Budget Awareness**: Keep users informed about their financial status through proactive notifications.

## Core Features
- **Expense Logging**: Quickly record expenses with relevant metadata (amount, date, category).
- **Summary Dashboard**: A central hub providing weekly and monthly spending summaries.
- **Budget Planning**: Allow users to set targets for fixed and variable expenses.
- **Smart Notifications**:
  - Alert when spending in a category exceeds the previous month's total.
  - Notify when spending exceeds the planned budget for either fixed or variable expenses.

## Constraints & Priorities
- **Academic Deadline**: Development is prioritized to meet the requirements and timelines of the university project.
- **Simplicity**: Focus on a clean, intuitive user interface that avoids unnecessary complexity.
